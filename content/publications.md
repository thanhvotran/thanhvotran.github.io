## M2NN: Multi-view multi-task graph neural network using congestion heatmap imagery for predicting traffic incidents across heterogeneous areas
<p class="meta">Transportation Research Part C: Emerging Technologies | 2026 | DOI: 10.1016/j.trc.2025.105458</p>
<div class="tags"><span class="tag">Open Access</span><span class="tag">Multimodal AI</span><span class="tag">Traffic Safety</span></div>

- Combines traffic data with congestion heatmap imagery.
- Uses graph, convolutional, and recurrent modelling components for spatiotemporal traffic incident forecasting.
- Jointly predicts link-level and area-level incident probabilities using a multi-task design.

<p class="links"><a href="https://doi.org/10.1016/j.trc.2025.105458">DOI</a> <a href="https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S0968090X25004620">ScienceDirect article</a> <a href="https://trid.trb.org/View/2633661">TRID record</a></p>

<section id="msgnn" class="publication">
## MSGNN: A Multi-structured Graph Neural Network model for real-time incident prediction in large traffic networks
<p class="meta">Transportation Research Part C: Emerging Technologies | 2023 | DOI: 10.1016/j.trc.2023.104354</p>
<div class="tags"><span class="tag">Open Access</span><span class="tag">Graph Neural Networks</span><span class="tag">Real-time Prediction</span></div>

- Predicts incident occurrence within sub-areas of a large traffic network.
- Uses multiple graph structures to fuse heterogeneous transport data sources.
- Evaluated across Brisbane and Gold Coast networks with 15-minute and 60-minute prediction horizons.

<p class="links"><a href="https://doi.org/10.1016/j.trc.2023.104354">DOI</a> <a href="https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S0968090X23003443">ScienceDirect article</a> <a href="https://trid.trb.org/View/2260145">TRID record</a></p>
