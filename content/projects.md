<section id="multimodal-deep-learning-research" class="project">
## Multimodal Deep Learning Research
<p class="meta">PhD Research and Publications</p>
<div class="tags"><span class="tag">Graph Neural Networks</span><span class="tag">Multimodal AI</span><span class="tag">Real-time Prediction</span></div>

- Built MSGNN, a multi-structured graph neural network for predicting traffic incident occurrence across large road networks.
- Represented each sub-area as graph inputs so the model could learn from road topology and heterogeneous data sources.
- Evaluated across Brisbane and Gold Coast networks at 15-minute and 60-minute prediction horizons.
- Developed M2NN, a multi-view, multi-task neural network combining sensor data and congestion heatmap imagery.
- Published open-access research in Transportation Research Part C.

</section>
